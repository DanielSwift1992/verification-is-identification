# Reading Is Order (Dynamics)

Existence Is Finite froze the static: Σ, COMPARE, and the verification corollary all hold timelessly, and Part III deferred the dynamics — "time as the track reading leaves, learning as the settling of order back into Σ — a separate study." This is that study. The agent is not a second space; it is the *active face* of the one space, a **reading** of the standing structure, and the reading is the one thing left free (the order, §5.25). The mathematics is already to hand: a reading-head is a **zipper** whose step is the derivative of the rule (R1); the two organs of a solve are **unfold and fold** — a coalgebra and an algebra, dual (R2); the verdict's indifference to order is **anamorphism uniqueness** (R3); the asymmetry of inevitability is **initiality** (R4); and crystallization is a μ-object precipitating from a ν-process (R5). The static is `μF`; the reading is the ν-side that deposits into it. Nothing new is postulated — the dynamics is the static, read.

## §0. Derivation

No new postulate. Everything below is read off `F(X) = 1 + X²`, its initial algebra `Σ = μF` (Existence Is Finite, Thm 1), its final coalgebra `νF`, and the order-is-sole-freedom result (V=I §5.25). The agent is the order of reading (EiF Part III); this document gives that order its shape.

## §1. The Reading-Head Is a Zipper

**R1 (The step is the derivative ∂F = 2X).** A position in a Σ-tree is a one-hole context, and for a polynomial functor the one-hole contexts are its *derivative* ∂F (McBride 2001). For `F(X) = 1 + X²`,

> ∂F(X) = ∂(1) + ∂(X²) = 0 + 2X = **2X**.

The hole sits in the left or the right child of a node — the **2** — carrying the sibling — the **X**. The reading-head is a **zipper** (Huet 1997): a focus, plus a stack of these contexts back to the root, `Zip(Σ) = Σ × (2 × Σ)*`. Moves are {down-left, down-right, up}; **time is the trace of the walk**. So the agent's local freedom is exactly **two — left or right, no third**: C5's binary distinction, read at the level of *motion* rather than of structure. **[solid]** (Huet 1997; McBride 2001; ∂(1+X²)=2X is arithmetic.)

## §2. The Two Organs Are Unfold and Fold

**R2 (Generator = coalgebra, discriminator = algebra; dual).** A **generator** is an F-*coalgebra* (A, c : A → F A) — *unfold*: from a state, stop or branch into two; its image lies in `νF`, the possible. A **discriminator** is the F-*algebra* structure of Σ — *fold*; COMPARE is its decidable equality; its home is `μF = Σ`, the actual. Algebra (F A → A) and coalgebra (A → F A) are **dual** — one arrow reversed — so the two organs of *Proposal Is Order* are the two directions of one functor: **generate = unfold (ν-ward), discriminate = fold (μ-ward)**, joined by the canonical map `μF → νF` that embeds the deposited actual into the generated possible. **[solid]** (Category theory; the algebra is Σ's structure map, EiF Thm 1.)

## §3. Order Is the Sole Freedom — Anamorphism Uniqueness

**R3 (The verdict is order-independent).** V=I §5.25 proved that, the encoding fixed, the only free variable is the order of COMPAREs, and correctness is order-invariant. Coalgebraically this is **finality**: from a generator (A, c) there is a *unique* anamorphism `A → νF`, independent of any traversal schedule. So any fair reading deposits the *same* limit; the order is free and alters no result — the dynamic carries no fact the static lacks. **[solid]** (Finality of νF; V=I §5.25 is its V=I face.) Time, then, is real but inert: it is the residue a reading leaves, never a term in the verdict.

## §4. The Asymmetry of Inevitability Is Initiality

**R4 (Initiality, not an adjunction).** `μF = Σ` is *initial*: a unique homomorphism *from* Σ *to* every F-algebra. So the fold/discriminator is **canonical** — one COMPARE, domain-independent, the same gate everywhere. F-coalgebras (A, c) are **many**, with no canonical choice: the unfold/generator is a *free parameter*, carrying the domain's alignment α. Two prior claims fall out:

- **Orthogonality** (Proposal Is Order GD3): a fixed constant (the gate) is independent of a free variable (the unfold), so every easy/hard quadrant inhabits.
- **The inevitability asymmetry** (GD4): *initiality is the inevitability* — the discriminator's structure is the unique initial algebra; the generator has no co-initial counterpart to be inevitable by.

The relation is **duality**, not an adjunction: there is no adjoint pair `gen ⊣ disc`, and the duality plus the initial/free asymmetry already yields both prior claims. **[solid** for the duality and the fixed/free asymmetry; **interpretive** for the one bridge — that categorical canonicity *is* GD4's information-theoretic inevitability ("at saturation the library teaches the ordering").]**

## §5. Crystallization Is μ From ν

**R5 (Learning settles ν into μ).** A reading is a ν-process — an unfold, possibly unbounded; each closed subterm **folds** (deposits) into `μF = Σ`. **Crystallization is the μ-object precipitating from the ν-process** — the Stefan front of Learning Is Liquid is the μ/ν boundary, solid behind, liquid ahead. Two faces:

- **Learning = convergence.** Saturation is the *colimit* of the deposit chain `X₀ → X₁ → ⋯`, each step folding new closed subterms into Σ: the directed union `⋃ₙ Xₙ = cl_G`, the least fixpoint of the closure operator (Basis Is Residue B8f). A fair reading reaches it, and the limit is **order-independent** — a directed union does not depend on the order of accumulation (Kleene). **[solid** given a fair reading; this is the saturation the draft left open.]**
- **The μ → ν flip.** A reading *with cycles* — draw-by-repetition, an agent that may go on forever — is genuinely ν: "can continue" is coinductive. Its value is a *greatest-fixpoint / retrograde* computation, not well-founded recursion; self-play is fixpoint iteration, and the cycle turns μ into ν. **[solid** that a cyclic value is a coinductive fixpoint; **interpretive** that this is "the flip."]**

## §6. Statement

1. The reading-head is a zipper; its step is the rule's derivative ∂F = 2X — two directions, no third (R1).
2. Generator and discriminator are unfold and fold — an F-coalgebra and an F-algebra, dual; generate is ν-ward, discriminate μ-ward (R2).
3. The verdict is order-independent: the anamorphism into νF is unique, so the dynamic adds order, not facts (R3).
4. The inevitability is the discriminator's because μF is initial; the generator is a free coalgebra-parameter — duality and initiality, not an adjunction (R4).
5. Crystallization is a μ-object precipitating from a ν-process; cyclic readings flip μ to ν (R5).

> **The static is the least fixpoint; the dynamic is its reading. The agent is the order in which the standing structure is read — a zipper walking Σ, unfolding candidates and folding verdicts — and the one freedom it has, the order, is the one thing that changes no result. Time is the track the reading leaves; learning is the track settling back into the space it never left.**
